Windows:

Power saving issue and you need to disable power management on your network adaptor(s):
1. Start Menu
2. Search for and open 'Control Panel'
3. Press "Enter"
4. Switch to Icon view (Small or Large, doesn't matter.)
5. Network and Sharing Center
6. Change adapter settings
7. Right-click the relevant adaptor
8. Properties (Accept UAC prompt if required)
9. Configure
10. Power Management
11. Tick/Untick the 'Allow the computer to turn off this device to save power'.
12. Click 'OK'
